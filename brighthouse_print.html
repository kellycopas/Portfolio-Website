BrightHouse Print Design
Previous Next

BrightHouse was the largest rent-to-own retailer in the UK with 245 stores. It provided home electronics, domestic appliances and household furniture to customers on affordable weekly payments.

During the Christmas period, BrightHouse created a digital advent calendar competition to engage customers and gave them a chance to win a prize everyday during December. To advertise this competition, I was tasked with designing a strut card that would sit next to tills in BrightHouse stores. I used Adobe InDesign to put the above design together.
