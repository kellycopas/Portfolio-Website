    • EARN MY STEPS

    A group UX research project investigating transport choices of students and staff at the University of the West of England. The task was to design a system to encourage people to choose a more sustainable option. The system design was required to meet at least one of the objectives of increasing physical activity and reducing unnecessary car trips. An interactive prototype was produced of an app that tracks the amount of steps and calories people take everyday to earn discounts for goods sold on campus.
